About Meet.AI

Meet.AI is the private AI backchannel that keeps you prepared in every conversation. We listen locally, never post to chat, and give you answers in the moment so you can stay focused on the person in front of you.

Last updated: 2025-10-22

Our Mission

Privacy-first collaboration

We believe professionals deserve real-time intelligence without surrendering meeting privacy. Meet.AI augments your conversations quietly—no recordings, no public transcripts, and no surprises for attendees.

Guiding Principles

Local by default

Audio is processed on your device when the mic is activated. Close Meet.AI and the session resets, leaving no residual chat or transcript.

Invisible presence

Meet.AI never posts to meeting chat. Responses arrive in your private window so you decide what to say aloud or share afterward.

Context with consent

Bring optional agendas or documents to sharpen answers. We encrypt context in transit and wipe it as soon as the session ends.

How Meet.AI Works Today

  1. Launch alongside your meeting. Open Meet.AI next to Google Meet or Zoom.
  2. Tap the mic when you need backup. Click once to let Meet.AI listen and click again to stop so it can return concise text suggestions.
  3. Respond with confidence. Use Meet.AI’s prompts, follow-ups, and quick facts to keep the conversation moving.
