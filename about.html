About Meeting Assistant

Last updated: 2025-10-22

Meeting Assistant is a private AI backchannel designed for live calls and meetings. You click the mic, let the other person speak, and the tool uses AI to provide a concise text answer that only you can see. It is not a meeting scribe and does not post or broadcast answers back into the meeting.

How it works

  • Listen & understand: Captures the other party’s question so the AI can interpret intent.
  • Get a private answer: Returns a short, actionable text answer for you to read silently.
  • Optional context: In supported versions, you can provide your own documents locally to ground the response.

What it is not

  • Not a recorder or public chatbot for meetings.
  • Does not share content with other participants.
  • Not a replacement for consent or company policies — use responsibly.
