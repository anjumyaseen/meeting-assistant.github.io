Meeting Assistant

Private AI backchannel for calls and meetings: click the mic, let the other person speak, and receive a concise text answer you can read silently. Answers are shown only to you — nothing is posted back into the meeting.

Note: When the Gemini page opens, Google may show “This app was created by another person.” That is normal for public Gemini projects — click Continue to proceed.

After signing in, you'll land in the Gemini app automatically.

What it does

Answer on the fly
AI drafts quick text answers while the other person asks the question.
Keep responses private
Results are visible only to you — not shared in the meeting.
Optional context
Provide a doc/agenda locally to ground answers (when supported in the app).
