Frequently Asked Questions

Answers to the most common questions about getting started with Meet.AI.

How fast can I get started with Meet.AI?

Sign in with Google, launch the companion window, and begin leveraging private suggestions in your next meeting. No extensions or admin approvals required.

Will other attendees know I’m using Meet.AI?

No. Meet.AI is invisible to participants. All prompts, summaries, and notes stay on your device unless you choose to share them.

What platforms does Meet.AI support today?

Meet.AI is tuned for Google Meet and Zoom and will expand to additional conferencing tools based on customer demand.

Can I customize responses with my team’s playbooks?

Yes. Upload optional context files securely to guide the assistant, and they are encrypted and cleared as soon as the session ends.

Does Meet.AI save any part of my session?

No. Meet.AI keeps everything transient. When you close the tool, the backchannel resets and nothing is stored for the next meeting.
